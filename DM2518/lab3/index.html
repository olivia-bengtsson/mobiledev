Feel Your Feet
    Stand on your feet. Experience the contact with the ground. Feel into the soles of the feet. Feel the knees. The hips. The spine. Shoulders. Neck. Head. Feel the connection between the top of the head and the feet.

Feel Your Shoulders
    Let your arms hang loose. Become aware of the shoulders. Slightly lift one shoulder and release it. Let the shoulder come all the way down. Lift the other shoulder and release it. Alternate three more times on each side. Experience the breath flowing in and out of the body.

Feel Your Face
    Become aware of your face. Feel the forehead. The eyebrows. Eyes. Nose. Nostrils. Air flowing through the nostrils. Cheeks. Jaw. Lips. Tongue. Whole of the head. The space surrounding the head.
